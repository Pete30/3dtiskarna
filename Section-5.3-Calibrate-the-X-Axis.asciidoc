* The X Bridge needs to be parallel to the Y-Rods. 
* Do this by putting any object on the right Y-Smooth Rod, turn the right motor spindle down by hand until it just touches the object.
* Do the same on the left side until both sides are equal.


|====
|image:media/Section_5_0025.png[width=600]
|====

* This calibration should be repeated after transport and or once a week or so. Make sure the Y-Rods are parallel first.
